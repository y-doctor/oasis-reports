OASIS — Potential False Negative Analysis (pFN Pool)

Per-construct binding-terminus classification of the 446-construct potential false negative (pFN) pool from the v2 iPSC ligand fitness screen. The pFN pool is defined as library constructs whose annotated cognate receptor is expressed in KOLF2.1J (FPKM ≥ 1.0) and whose specific barcode never reached FDR < 0.05 in any of the six vs-Day-4 screens. Full upstream method, hit calling, and pool census are in the main binding-terminus report.
